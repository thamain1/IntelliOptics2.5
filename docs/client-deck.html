The Solution

Four Integrated Capabilities

Intelligent Detection

Identifies objects, people, vehicles, and events. Custom detection rules built for your operation. Fires alerts the moment something occurs — with bounding box visualization on every detection.

Natural Language Analysis

Ask your cameras questions in plain English. "Is the gate closed?" "Is anyone in the restricted zone?" Reads text, signs, and plates directly from live feeds.

Camera Health Monitoring

Inspects every camera automatically day and night. Detects blur, darkness, overexposure, and misalignment. Alerts your team before a coverage gap becomes a liability.

Operational Intelligence

Real-time dashboards across your camera estate. Automated reports on your schedule. Audit logs ready for compliance. Connects to existing workflows without disruption.
